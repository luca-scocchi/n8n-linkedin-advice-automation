# Overview

This n8n workflow automates the discovery and analysis of LinkedIn Advice articles and generates original AI-powered contributions.

It is designed for professionals, creators, and teams who want to stay visible on LinkedIn by consistently providing high-quality insights without manually searching for articles or drafting responses.

The workflow focuses on:
- Topic-driven article discovery
- Structured content extraction
- Original, non-repetitive AI contributions
- Human-in-the-loop delivery

It can be used both as a production automation or as a reference architecture for AI-driven workflows in n8n.
